Grammar: Age

How do we say our age in English? This is a very common mistake!

"I have 20 years." (WRONG in English!)

"I am 20 years old." (Correct)

Select the correct rule:

RULE UNLOCKED!

Never use "have" for age! Always use the Verb To Be: I am, He is, She is.

Teacher: Ask Student: "How old are you?"
